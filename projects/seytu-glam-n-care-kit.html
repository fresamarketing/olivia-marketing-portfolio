CASE STUDY / 05

SEYTÚ Glam N Care
Kit Campaign

A product and launch-support project created to introduce the SEYTÚ Glam N Care Kit through branded collateral, educational materials, and customer-facing launch assets.

Project overview

Launch materials
with a clear purpose.

This project focused on creating and organizing launch support materials for the SEYTÚ Glam N Care Kit. The work included promotional mockups, educational one-pagers, and customer-facing support materials designed to help present the kit clearly and professionally.

The goal

More than
a product bundle.

The goal was to package the Glam N Care Kit as more than a product bundle by giving it a clear presentation, a polished identity, and helpful materials that explained what was included, how it supported customer engagement, and how it connected to business-building efforts.

My role

I supported the visual development and presentation of the kit by helping create mockups, educational collateral, and branded support materials. My work focused on organizing content clearly, aligning the materials to the brand, and helping translate the kit into a more polished customer-facing experience.

  • Product Marketing

  • Campaign Support

What I created

From mockup
to support materials.

From concept to live experience

Customer-facing
shopping experience.

The project evolved from early kit concepts and educational collateral into a customer-facing product experience on the SEYTÚ U.S. website. The final product page presented the kit contents, supporting materials, product imagery, and business purpose in a clear and accessible shopping format.

The early Glam N Care Kit collateral featured a broader nine-product concept, while the final website implementation featured three core beauty products and additional support materials.

A more polished
kit experience.

The final materials helped position the Glam N Care Kit as a more complete and polished experience. By combining product presentation with educational and support collateral, the project made the kit easier to understand, easier to share, and more aligned with the brand.
